Tennis
♦ A game in which two or four players strike a ball with rackets over a net stretched across a court.
In Comparison to Golf
♦ Similar to the reputation golf has, tennis is a social sport that people of all ages can participate in. BUT unlike golf, tennis requires endurance and constant body movement in every point, including when waiting for your oponent to return the ball.

Pros/Cons of Being A Tennis Player

Pros Cons
Physical Advantages
  • Reduced risk of heart disease and diabities
  • Strengthens your bones
  • Helps control weight
Physical Disadvantages
  • High-demand on body
  • Requires strength and endurance
  • Possible injures: inflammation of the shoulder/knee joints. tendinities, tennis elbow, pulls and tears
Mental Advantages
  • Helps build self-confidence
  • Tension and stress reliever
  • Helps maintain cognitive abilities in adults and seniors
  • Improves mental awareness
  • Improves assertiveness
  • Helps develop self-control
  • Allows for continuous brain development
Expense Disadvantages
  • May have to pay membership to use courts
  • Equipement such as racket and proper attire can be costly
Social Advantages
  • Great way to meet people
  • More time to spend with friends
  • Regardless of age or skill level, normally pretty easy to find a competetive match
  • Doubles allows for bettering communication skills
  • Strengthens relationships on and off the court
As you can see, there are many more pros than cons to playing tennis. To summarize, starting to play tennis at a young age can be beneficial to your overall health, not only in the present, but in the future as well. Tennis can allow anyone of any age to interact and form relationships with others. Not only can tennis help form relationships, but it can also help improve existing relationships, such as one between a husband and wife or mother and daughter, and so on. While tennis can be a highly competitive sport, it can also provide a relaxed, carefree environment. On and off the court people interact with their team and their opponents which allows for the opportunity to build relationships within your community and outside of a work environment. The pros outweigh the cons, although injuries are always possibility depending on the level and amount a person plays. It is essential to have a balanced lifestyle that ensures that you can enjoy yourself while making a positive impact on your body.
